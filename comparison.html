48-Hour SLA Windows

Two critical SLA windows are enforced: Job TD to signed field ticket returned and signed ticket receipt to invoice submission. System alerts go to Ashby, Finance, and Operations if either 48-hour window is breached.

Existing Customers

For repeat work with an existing customer, the process skips Phase 1 and starts directly at Phase 2 — Work Is Ordered.
